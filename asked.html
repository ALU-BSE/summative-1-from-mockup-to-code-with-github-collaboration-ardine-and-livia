Get to Know Gasabo
Gasabo District is one of the 3 Districts that make up the City of
Kigali. It is located in the northeast of Kigali City. The District
Headquarters is located in Remera Sector, in Nyarutarama Cell.
Gasabo District is bordered by two Regions: Kicukiro: in the South.
Accommodation in the North. Refuge: in the East. Nyarugenge:
in the west
